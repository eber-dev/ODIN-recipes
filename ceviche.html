How to prepare Ceviche

CLASSIC CEVICHE (Peruvian Style)

Ingredients:

  • 500 g (about 1 lb) fresh white fish fillet (such as sea bass, tilapia, flounder, or cod)
  • 1 red onion
  • Juice of 10 limes
  • 1 chili pepper
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper to taste
  • Cooked corn
  • Cooked sweet potato
  • Lettuce leaves

Preparation:

  1. Cut the fish into small bite-sized cubes and place them in a glass or stainless steel bowl.
  2. Season with salt, pepper, garlic, and chili. Mix gently.
  3. Squeeze the limes and pour the fresh juice over the fish until itâ€™s completely covered.
  4. Add the sliced red onion and chopped cilantro. Stir gently to combine.
  5. Let it marinate in the fridge for 10 to 15 minutes (no more than 20 minutes, or the fish may become too firm or overcooked by the lime).
  6. Serve chilled, with cooked corn, sweet potato slices, and optionally on a bed of lettuce.
